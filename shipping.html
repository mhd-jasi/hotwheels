SHIPPING POLICY

Delivery Timeline

Orders are processed and shipped within 2-3 business days. Delivery timelines may vary based on your location and courier services. Standard delivery usually takes 5-7 business days.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout and are based on the total weight of your order and the delivery location. Free shipping is available on orders above a specified amount, as mentioned on our website.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, a tracking number will be provided to you via email or SMS. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment.

International Shipping

We currently ship to select international locations. Please note that international shipments may be subject to additional customs fees or import duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to incorrect delivery information or failure to accept delivery, a re-shipping fee may apply. Please ensure your shipping address is accurate at the time of checkout.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our shipping policy, please feel free to contact our customer support team at contact us.
